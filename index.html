FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

How do you recreate my avatar?
Just give us your Roblox username at checkout. We look it up and 3D print a figure that matches your exact look. We never need your password.
What is the difference between 2 inch and 3 inch?
The 2 inch figure ($45) is compact and great for a desk or shelf. The 3 inch ($60) is larger with more visible detail — ideal for collectors who want every feature to stand out.
How long does shipping take?
Production takes 3–5 business days, and shipping takes 4–7 business days. Total estimated time is 7–12 business days. We ship worldwide for free.
What if I'm not happy with my order?
Contact us and we will work to make it right. Custom items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or do not match your avatar.
Can I pay by credit or debit card?
Yes! When the PayPal popup opens, click "Pay with Debit or Credit Card" at the bottom. No PayPal account needed. Visa, Mastercard, and Amex are all accepted. Your card details go directly to PayPal — we never see them.
What if I change my avatar after ordering?
Email us within 24 hours of placing your order. After that we may have already started printing and cannot change the design.

Refund Policy

Custom 3D printed figures cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or do not match your avatar. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order.

Shipping Policy

Production 3–5 days, shipping 4–7 days. Total: 7–12 business days. Free worldwide shipping. Tracking provided when available.

Privacy Policy

Your data is used only to fulfill your order. We never sell or share it. Payment is handled entirely by PayPal — we never see your card details.
