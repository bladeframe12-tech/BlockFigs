FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering.

How do you recreate my avatar?
You provide your Roblox username at checkout. We look up your avatar in-game and 3D print a figure that matches your exact look — outfit, colors, accessories, everything. We do not need your password.
What is the figure made of?
Each figure is 3D printed using high quality materials with precise detail. The figure stands 3 inches (7.6cm) tall and comes in a protective display box.
How long does shipping take?
Production takes 3–5 business days, shipping takes 4–7 more days. Total: approximately 7–10 business days from order to delivery. We ship worldwide.
What if I change my avatar after ordering?
We capture your avatar at the time of order. If you want a different look, contact us within 24 hours at mounirbenji12@gmail.com and we can make changes before printing begins.
What if I am not happy with my order?
If your figure does not match your avatar or arrives damaged, contact us and we will send a replacement or give you a full refund. Your satisfaction is guaranteed.
