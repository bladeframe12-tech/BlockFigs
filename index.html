FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering.

How do you recreate my avatar?
Just give us your Roblox username at checkout. We look it up and 3D print a figure that matches your exact look. We never need your password.
What is the difference between 2 inch and 3 inch?
The 2 inch figure ($45) is compact and great for a desk or shelf. The 3 inch figure ($60) is our larger premium option with more detail.
How long does shipping take?
Production takes 3-5 business days. Shipping takes 4-7 business days. Total estimated delivery: 7-12 business days. We ship worldwide.
What if I am not happy with my order?
Contact us at mounirbenji12@gmail.com and we will work to make it right. Custom items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or do not match the avatar.
What if I change my avatar after ordering?
Email us within 24 hours at mounirbenji12@gmail.com. After that we may have already started printing.
Can I pay by credit card?
Yes. When the PayPal window opens you will see a "Pay with Debit or Credit Card" option at the bottom. No PayPal account is needed. Your card info goes directly to PayPal — we never see it.

Refund Policy

Custom 3D printed figures cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or do not match your avatar. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order at mounirbenji12@gmail.com.

Shipping Policy

Production: 3-5 business days. Shipping: 4-7 business days. Total estimated: 7-12 business days. We ship worldwide for free. Tracking info provided when available.

Privacy Policy

Your name, email, and shipping address are used only to fulfill your order. We do not sell or share your data. Payment info is handled entirely by PayPal — we never see your card details.
