FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

How do you recreate my avatar?
Just give us your Roblox username at checkout. We look it up and 3D print a figure that matches your exact look. We never need your password.
What is the difference between 2 inch and 3 inch?
The 2 inch figure ($45) is compact and great for a desk or shelf. The 3 inch ($60) is larger with more visible detail — ideal for collectors.
How long does shipping take?
Production: 3–5 business days. Shipping: 4–7 business days. Total: 7–12 business days. We ship worldwide for free.
What if I'm not happy with my order?
Contact us and we will make it right. Custom items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or don't match your avatar.
Can I pay by credit or debit card?
Yes! When the PayPal window opens, click "Pay with Debit or Credit Card." No PayPal account needed — Visa, Mastercard, and Amex all accepted. We never see your card details.

Refund Policy

Custom 3D printed figures cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or do not match your avatar. Contact us within 7 days of receiving your order.

Shipping Policy

Production 3–5 days, shipping 4–7 days. Total: 7–12 business days. Worldwide free shipping. Tracking provided when available.

Privacy Policy

Your data is used only to fulfill your order. We never sell or share it. Payment is handled entirely by PayPal — we never see your card details.
