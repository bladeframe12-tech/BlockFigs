FAQ

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before ordering.

How exactly do you recreate my avatar?
You give us your Roblox username and we manually look up your avatar in-game. Our artists then paint and sculpt the figure to match your exact look - including outfit colors, accessories, hair, and body type. We do not need your password or any login access.
What if I change my avatar after ordering?
We capture your avatar's appearance at the time of order. If you update your avatar after purchasing and want the new look, please contact us within 24 hours of ordering - we can accommodate changes before production begins.
How long does shipping take?
Production takes 3–5 business days, and shipping takes an additional 4–7 business days depending on your location. Total time from order to delivery is typically 7–10 business days. We ship worldwide!
What is the figure made of?
Each figure is made from high-quality PVC/resin and painted with UV-resistant acrylic paints. The figure is 3 inches (7.6cm) tall and comes in a foam-padded collectible display box.
What if I'm not happy with my figure?
We stand behind every figure we make. If your figure doesn't match your avatar or arrives damaged, contact us and we'll make it right - either with a replacement or a full refund. Customer satisfaction is our priority.
Is this store affiliated with Roblox?
No. BlockFigs is an independent store and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Roblox Corporation. We are fans creating fan-made collectibles for the community.
