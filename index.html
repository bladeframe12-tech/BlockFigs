FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

How do you recreate my avatar?
Just give us your Roblox username at checkout. We look it up and 3D print a figure that matches your exact look. We never need your password.
2 inch vs 3 inch — what's the difference?
The 2 inch ($45) is compact, great for a desk or shelf. The 3 inch ($60) is larger with more visible detail — ideal for collectors.
How long does shipping take?
Production: 3–5 business days. Shipping: 4–7 business days. Total: 7–12 business days. We ship worldwide for free.
Will real money be charged from my card or PayPal?
Yes — this is a real PayPal Live payment. Money is charged immediately when you approve it. You will receive a PayPal receipt in your email. Your card data goes directly to PayPal — we never see it.
Can I pay by credit or debit card without a PayPal account?
Yes! When the PayPal window opens, click "Pay with Debit or Credit Card" at the bottom. Enter your card details there. No PayPal account required.
What if I'm not happy with my order?
Contact us and we will make it right. Custom items cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or do not match your avatar.

Refund Policy

Shipping Policy

Production 3–5 days, shipping 4–7 days. Total 7–12 business days. Free worldwide shipping. Tracking provided when available.

Privacy Policy

Your data is used only to fulfill your order. Payment is handled entirely by PayPal — we never see your card details.
